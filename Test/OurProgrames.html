    What is heartfulness Meditation ?

    Heartfulness is a way of living from the heart, allowing the mind to relax and unwind. Heartfulness Meditation is a process of naturally going deep within one's heart and connecting with one's inner presence. It is a simple and practical technique that one can use alongside any other form of yoga, meditation or spiritual practice.
    Within just a few months of practice, our mind gets progressively calmer and the connection with the heart becomes lighter and more open.
    b>

    What kind of meditation do you do?

    It is a heart-based silent meditation where we learn to gently turn our attention towards our heart to experience the inner presence for ourselves.
